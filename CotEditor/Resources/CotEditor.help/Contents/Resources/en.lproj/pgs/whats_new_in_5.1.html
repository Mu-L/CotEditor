What’s new in CotEditor 5.1 on Mac

Easier to destinguish documents
Unique folder names appended to the window title make it easy to distinguish between documents with the same filename.
Protect documents from unwanted changes
To prevent accidental editing, you can make documents read-only by the Format > Prevent Editing command or the cot command-line tool.
